About your guide

Jeff was born and raised in Southern/Central Maine, since he was old enough to hold a fishing pole he has been chasing fish. In 1998, when he was in his early 20's, Jeff obtained his Registered Maine Guide license. At the time, raising his family and work obligations prevented him from doing the guiding he wanted to do. However, as life has settled down Jeff is now ready to take people out and share his passion for fishing, and hopefully catch a few fish along the way. With years of fishing experience, he has his favorite spots to share and is constantly searching new waters for the next big catch.

Things to bring

  • Fishing license
  • Appropriate clothes for season/weather-rain coat, gloves, sandals in summer boots in cooler weather, etc...
  • Sunscreen
  • Hat with visor
  • Sunglasses
  • Camera
  • Sense of adventure and positive vibes
